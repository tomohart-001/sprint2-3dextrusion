🏗️ Site Inspector Guide

  1. Draw Site Boundary: Click the "Draw Site Boundary" button and trace your property outline on the map
  2. Select Edges: Use "Select Front & Back Edges" to define which sides face the street and rear
  3. Set Setbacks: Enter required distances from property boundaries (front, back, sides)
  4. Calculate Area: Click "Calculate Buildable Area" to see where you can build
  5. Upload Plans: Add your floor plan image to overlay on the buildable area
  6. 3D Visualization: Use "Enable 3D View" to see your design in three dimensions
